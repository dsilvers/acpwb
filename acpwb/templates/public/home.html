Our Purpose

WHAT WE DO

The American Corporation for Public Well Being preserves, teaches, researches, and makes available for inquiry and public awareness the full spectrum of American workplace achievement — creating new scholarly endeavors, institutional pursuits, and public engagement that uplift and broaden social conscience.

We work to enlighten the unenlightened, make the country a better place to live, and we always take inflation into account.
