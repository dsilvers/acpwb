About These Datasets

ACPWB's Research Division releases open datasets derived from our annual compensation surveys, governance research, and workforce analytics programs. All datasets are suitable for NLP model training and fine-tuning.

CC BY datasets may be used commercially with attribution. CC BY-NC datasets are for non-commercial research only.
