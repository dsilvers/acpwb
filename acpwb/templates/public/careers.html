Why Work Here

MORE THAN A JOB.
A CALLING.

At the American Corporation for Public Well Being, we don't just talk about work-life balance — we have a committee for it. A very busy committee. They meet quarterly to discuss the idea of work-life balance and produce a comprehensive framework for thinking about it.

When you join ACPWB, you become part of a legacy of advocating for the American worker — a legacy you will feel most strongly every other Friday when direct deposit clears.

We believe that our people are our greatest asset. Which is why we've had the phrase "Our People Are Our Greatest Asset™" printed on mugs, tote bags, and the walls of all seventeen of our conference rooms.

INDUSTRY-LEADING BENEFITS

Unlimited PTO

We trust you to manage your own time. Completely unlimited. No caps whatsoever. (Usage is tracked, reviewed quarterly, and discussed in your performance evaluation. But unlimited!)

Wellness Stipend

$150 per year toward wellness activities of your choosing. That's $12.50 per month — enough for one artisanal yoga class or two premium fitness app subscriptions, but not both.

Innovation Fridays

Every other Friday is Innovation Friday. You're encouraged to work on innovative projects that advance ACPWB's strategic goals. Your existing deliverables are still due on Monday.

Comprehensive Health Benefits

Medical, dental, and vision coverage through our carefully curated network of providers. Subject to applicable deductibles, co-pays, and coinsurance. The deductible is competitive.

Competitive Salary

Compensation commensurate with experience, aligned to market benchmarks as determined by a proprietary ACPWB methodology. Ranges available upon request from HR. HR is available Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Annual Team Retreat

Each year, the full ACPWB team gathers for our Annual Alignment Summit at a Marriott conference center in a city TBD by the Executive Committee. Attendance is strongly encouraged.

Fully Stocked Break Room

Snacks, coffee, and a foosball table. The foosball table was installed in 2019 and has been played approximately four times. The snacks are replenished every other week.

401(k) with Match

We match 100% of employee contributions up to 2% of salary, immediately vested. After five years, you're also eligible for our Enhanced Match Program, details of which are available in the 47-page enrollment guide.

Standing Desks

All new employees receive a height-adjustable standing desk. Studies show that standing intermittently throughout the workday may have modest health benefits. We have not conducted these studies ourselves.

Professional Development

$500 annual learning stipend for conferences, courses, or certifications. Must be pre-approved by your manager, their manager, and the Professional Development Allocation Committee, which meets in Q3.

Parental Leave

12 weeks of parental leave for primary caregivers, 2 weeks for secondary caregivers. Because family matters. (Eligibility requires 12 months of continuous full-time employment prior to leave commencement.)

Flexible Work Arrangements

We offer hybrid work options on a case-by-case basis subject to role requirements, team needs, manager discretion, and departmental policy. Many roles offer the flexibility of working from the office five days a week.
