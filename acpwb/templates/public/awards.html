Our Track Record

RECOGNIZED FOR WHAT
WE ALREADY KNEW.

Since our founding in 2006, the American Corporation for Public Well Being has received recognition from a wide range of industry bodies, regional business groups, trade associations, and self-assessment frameworks. We are proud of each and every award, especially the ones we nominated ourselves for.

Our awards span multiple disciplines: workforce analytics, corporate governance, ESG reporting, compensation transparency, and general excellence in the broad area of being an excellent organization. Many of these awards come with physical plaques, which are displayed in our Milwaukee headquarters lobby.

We believe recognition matters — not because we need external validation, but because external validation is very nice to have and looks good on proposals. Our award shelf is currently at 73% capacity. We are working on this.

FEATURED RECOGNITIONS

2024 — Workforce Analytics
National Excellence in Compensation Transparency Award

Awarded by the American Institute for Compensation Research and Adjacent Fields

Presented annually to the organization demonstrating the most sustained commitment to compensation benchmarking methodology, data integrity, and the production of reports that people actually read. ACPWB received this award in 2024, 2022, and 2019. We did not receive it in 2020 or 2021 and consider those years a growth opportunity.

2024 — Workplace Culture
Wisconsin's Top 50 Employers — Midsize Organization Category

Wisconsin Business Forward Annual Rankings

ACPWB has appeared on this list six times in the past eight years, making us technically a perennial honoree, though the magazine has asked us not to use that phrase in marketing materials without clarifying the two-year gap. We respect this. The ranking is based on employee satisfaction surveys, benefit quality, and parking availability.

2023 — ESG Leadership
Gold Tier — Corporate Governance & ESG Reporting Framework

Midwest Corporate Sustainability Consortium

Gold Tier certification recognizes organizations that have completed all required ESG self-reporting modules, submitted documentation on time, and paid the consortium's annual membership fee at the Gold level. ACPWB has held Gold Tier status since 2017. Silver Tier was also available; we chose Gold.

2023 — Research & Data
Most Trusted Compensation Dataset — Reader's Choice

HR Technology & Analytics Quarterly

Voted by readers of HR Technology & Analytics Quarterly in their annual survey of 2,400 HR and finance professionals. ACPWB received 31% of votes in the compensation benchmarking category, narrowly defeating three competitors whose names we are not permitted to mention per the magazine's editorial policy.
